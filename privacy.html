Privacy Policy

mochi-pipeline — the Momo & Chi publishing tool · Effective date: July 16, 2026

1. Overview

mochi-pipeline is a personal, single-user tool that runs locally on the owner's own machine. It is not offered as a service to anyone else, and it does not collect data from any third party.

2. What it stores

The tool stores only the owner's own data, locally on the owner's machine:

  • OAuth access and refresh tokens for the owner's own YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok accounts (stored encrypted at rest);
  • the owner's own video files, captions, and related metadata.

Nothing else is stored.

3. No third-party data

The tool does not collect, receive, process, or store personal data belonging to any other person. There are no end users other than the owner.

4. No cookies, analytics, or tracking

The tool sets no cookies and runs no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.

5. No selling or sharing

Data is never sold, rented, or shared with anyone. The stored tokens are used solely to publish the owner's own content to the owner's own accounts through the platforms' official APIs.

6. Data location and control

All data is stored locally on the owner's machine. The owner can delete all stored data — tokens, video files, and the local database — at any time, which permanently removes it.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to momoandchi.studio@gmail.com.
